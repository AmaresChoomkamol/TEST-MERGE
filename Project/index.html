Our Agency

We give U.S. leaders the intelligence they need to keep our
country safe

As the world’s premier foreign intelligence agency, the work we
do at CIA is vital to U.S. national security. We collect and
analyze foreign intelligence and conduct covert action. U.S.
policymakers, including the President of the United States,
make policy decisions informed by the information we provide.

Our Legacy

CIA’s legacy is one of brave individuals
dedicated to freedom, fulfilling our intelligence
mission with ingenuity and grit

Protecting our country is at the heart of our
mission — past, present, and future. From our
World War II roots through today, we carry on the
traditions of ingenuity, bravery, and quiet sacrifice
that have made us the world’s premier foreign
intelligence agency.
